A note on observation

"The best record of a thing is the one that interferes with it least. We watch in order to understand, never to surveil."

Spectator captures the structure of a page and every change made to it — additions, removals, the smallest text edits — and reconstructs the visit in a sandboxed iframe. Form fields marked data-spectator-mask never leave the browser intact: card numbers, expiries and security codes are replaced with bullets at capture time, before any byte is sent to the server.
